CT Cyclic Loading Fixture

This is a 5000lb cyclic loading fixture designed for Mytra's Cell Tray (CT) validation and verification testing. As the V&V team's first permanent fixture, it was built to validate FEA models and verify production quality through high-cycle fatigue testing at 200k cycles per test.

The fixture features a robust 3"x3" stainless steel square tube frame designed to withstand 5000lb loading conditions for 5-10 years and 10M+ cycles. Dual hydraulic actuators provide precise linear actuation with custom mounting feet, retaining geometry, and mechanical stops.

The control system integrates in-line pressure transducers, SMT-32 dev boards, and solenoid hydraulic valves. I managed the complete design process including trade studies, supplier interfaces, and a $40k BOM with components from JLCNC and Oshcut. Approximately $15k of hydraulic infrastructure is reusable for future fixtures.
