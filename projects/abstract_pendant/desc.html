abstract_pendant

This is an abstract pendant designed for Stanford's FashionX Jewelry Team. The necklace was modeled in Blender for greater felxibility.

After being designed, the piece was 3D printed in castable wax and sent to a silversmith to be cast in silver.

On return, I finished the piece and added a chain so it could be worn as jewelry.
