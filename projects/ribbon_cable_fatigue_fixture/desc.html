Ribbon Cable Fatigue Fixture

This semi-permanent fixture validates and characterizes ribbon cable failure rates at different bend radii, critical for ensuring reliability in Mytra's robotic systems that experience continuous motion cycles.

The fixture consists of four custom 3D-printed brackets mounted to a Zaber linear actuator and Thorlabs optical breadboard. Testing at bend radii of 20mm, 25mm, and 30mm revealed that cable lifespan increased dramatically from ~45k cycles to ~200k cycles with larger radii, approaching the copper's fatigue strength limit.

This data directly informed design decisions for cable routing throughout the robot, balancing space constraints with reliability requirements. The modular design allows for quick radius changes and testing of different cable configurations.
